NCSM Role in Innovation Hub Scheme

The National Council of Science Museums (NCSM) serves as the implementing agency for the Innovation Hub scheme, operating under the administrative control of the Ministry of Culture.

Scheme: 'Innovation Hub'

Launched by NCSM to create dedicated spaces that foster innovation, creativity, and scientific thinking among students, researchers, and the general public.
