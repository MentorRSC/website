Bubble Sort Algorithm: The autoplay uses the Bubble Sort algorithm which repeatedly steps through the list, compares adjacent elements and swaps them if they are in the wrong order.

How to Play

  1. Select two cards. The selected card turns red.
  2. If you want to exchange the selected card, press the [Switch] button.
  3. If you think all the cards are in order, press the [Flip All] button to confirm.
  4. Try to complete with a minimum number of moves.
  5. Use Autoplay to watch the computer solve the puzzle automatically.
Autoplay Colors:
  • Blue: Scanning through cards
  • Yellow: Comparing two cards
  • Purple: Swapping cards
  • Green: Cards in correct position
